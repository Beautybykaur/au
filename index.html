Why to choose us

WAXING

Waxing is a popular hair removal method that also exfoliates the skin, providing smoother and softer skin. It removes microorganisms, upper skin layers, blood, and wound scabs, which can harbor harmful bacteria or pathogens. However, incorrect waxing can cause skin irritation, inflammation, and infections. It is recommended to seek professional help or follow proper hygiene protocols to minimize the risk of any adverse effects.

PEELS

A chemical peel is a cosmetic treatment in which a chemical solution is applied to the face to remove dead skin cells and stimulate the growth of new cells.
3 different peels from Dermalogica, each one specifically designed for instant: Problematic,pigmentation and Dehydration.
The objective is to enhance the appearance of the skin by reducing age spots, fine lines, and wrinkles and uneven out skin tone. Chemical peels are classified into three types - superficial, medium, and deep - based on the depth of penetration into the skin. It is essential to seek professional help for chemical peels to avoid any adverse effects and achieve the desired results.
